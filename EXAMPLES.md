# Usage Examples

## Example 1: Compare Tech Stocks

Compare Apple and Microsoft:

1. Enter OpenAI API key
2. Stock 1: `AAPL`
3. Stock 2: `MSFT`
4. Click "Analyze Stocks"

## Example 2: Compare Different Sectors

Compare a tech stock with a finance stock:

1. Stock 1: `GOOGL` (Google)
2. Stock 2: `JPM` (JPMorgan)

## Example 3: Compare Competitors

Compare Tesla and Ford:

1. Stock 1: `TSLA`
2. Stock 2: `F`

## Tips

- Use uppercase for stock symbols
- Valid symbols are 1-5 characters
- The analysis includes price, fundamentals, and recommendations
